Cards Make It Easy to Create and Share Community Resources

Taryn decides to try creating a card and giving it out to her neighbors. She has them scan a QR code at the local block party, and they use biometric confirmation on their phones to accept it. Now it's easy for anyone to create a place anywhere on the internet that's only accessible to people from the neighborhood.

One neighbor decides to create a neighbors-only list of free stuff. Another creates a community calendar that requires a neighborhood card to view and post. A kid decides to create a neighbors-only Minecraft server. When someone loads up the server they just need to drag their card from a wallet to the login request to enter.
