What's a Membership Card?

A membership card is a digital id that communities can give out to prove that people are trusted. Think about a library card, a union card, or being a "card-carrying member" of a community. A card can be used to access exclusive information, conversations, and resources that a community stores anywhere on the internet. If you choose to show a card, it offers strong proof that you are trusted by a community. If you choose not to show it, no one knows that it exists.
